## 2. OBJECTIVE

Investigate whether neutrino flavor states exhibit internal substructure or additional states beyond the standard 3ν model, as predicted by the gyroscopic moment mechanism in NHC/TD theory.

**Primary Questions:**
1. Do neutrino oscillation parameters show flavor-dependent variations?
2. Are there additional peaks or substructures in neutrino energy spectra?
3. Do neutrino velocities differ by flavor/gyroscopic moment group?
4. Is there a latent structure in neutrino data not explained by standard 3ν model?

## 5. SCOPE

### In Scope:
- Analysis of published neutrino oscillation data
- Search for flavor-dependent variations
- Blind search for latent structures
- Comparison with standard 3ν model

### Out of Scope:
- New data collection (only published data)
- Modifications to NHC/TD theory (only testing)
- Claims of "discovery" without statistical significance

---

## 6. SUCCESS CRITERIA

**Primary:**
- Statistical evidence (BF > 3 or p < 0.05) for flavor-dependent variations OR
- Detection of latent structure not explained by 3ν model

**Secondary:**
- Upper limits on flavor-dependent effects
- Identification of systematic uncertainties

**Failure:**
- No statistically significant deviations from 3ν model
- All observed effects explainable by systematics
